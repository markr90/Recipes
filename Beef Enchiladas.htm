Beef Enchiladas

1

Preheat oven to 175C. Heat oil in saucepan, add beef and stir until cooked. Cut onion and bell pepper <3mm. Mince garlic and coriander. Add onion, bell pepper, spices. Cook slowly for another 20 minutes, until vegetables are soft and flavors unite.

2

Heat tortillas for 30sec in microwave (until soft so they can be folded easily). Coat with enchilada sauce. Cover with 1 tbsp of cheese, add 2 tbsp of beef mixture (use slotted spoon!), and roll mixture in tortilla.

3

Place side to side in a heat resistant dish (in my wegdewood dish cut about 1 cm from each side of the tortilla to fit). Spread enchilada sauce over tortillas and spread cheese evenly. If desired spread sliced black olives for decoration.

4

Heat in oven until surface starts to bubble, about 30 minutes.

Servings: 4

Yield: 12 pcs
