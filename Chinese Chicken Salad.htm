1

Marinate chicken in teryakisauce for an hour or so. Preheat even to 175C. Roast chicken for 45 minutes covered with foil (alternative: use grilled chicken thighs as you can buy it from the butcher, but this is not so nice.). Toast sesame seeds in a dry pan (alternative, use cashews, peanuts). Let cool to room temperature.

2

Mix sesame oil, vegetable oil, sugar, pepper, salt, vinegar and mustard powder to a smooth dressing. Cut chicken in bite size pieces. Deep fry rice noodles or shrimp crackers

3

Cut salad in thin (5mm) stripes, no longer than 4 cm. Cut spring onions diagonally in 5mm slices. Clean cilantro leaves. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl with dressing just before serving.

4

Add chicken pieces and fried shrimp crackers or rice noodles on top. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Servings: 4
