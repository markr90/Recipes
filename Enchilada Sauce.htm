Sautee onions in oil. When tender add garlic, and a little later tomato puree. Sautee for a minute on medium heat and then add the canned tomatoes, spices, bouillon cube and 200ml water. Simmer for 30 minutes to unite flavors. Bring to taste with pepper and salt.
